Meet Coach Peter Strucinski

With over 20 years of experience in professional table tennis, Coach Peter Strucinski has dedicated his life to developing champions. His unique training methodology combines technical precision, tactical awareness, and mental fortitude.

Peter Strucinski's Table Tennis Academy (TTA) has produced numerous national and international champions. Our state-of-the-art facilities and personalized training programs are designed to help players of all levels achieve their maximum potential.
