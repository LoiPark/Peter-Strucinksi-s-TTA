Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our training programs

We train only people who are serious about improving their game. If you are not serious about improving your game, please do not waste your time and money.

Private sessions are typically 60 minutes, while group training sessions last 90 minutes.

We recommend training 2-3 times per week for recreational players and 4-5 times per week for competitive players. Consistency is key to improvement. We'll help you create a training schedule that fits your goals and availability.

Peter Strucinski's TTA combines world-class coaching expertise with personalized attention.
