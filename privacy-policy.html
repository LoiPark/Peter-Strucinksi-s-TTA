Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 22, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Coach Peter ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you visit or interact with this website.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

a. Information You Provide Voluntarily

When you submit a contact form or otherwise communicate with us, you may provide:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Message content
  • Any other information you choose to share

We collect this information solely for communication and service-related purposes.

b. Automatically Collected Information

When you visit this website, certain non-personal information may be collected automatically, such as:

  • Browser type
  • Device type
  • Pages visited
  • Time spent on the site

This information is used only to understand website usage and improve the site experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to inquiries and messages
  • Communicate about coaching services
  • Improve website functionality and content
  • Maintain website security

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

3. Third-Party Services

This website may use or embed third-party services, including but not limited to:

  • YouTube (for embedded videos)
  • Contact form services (such as Formspree)
  • Analytics tools (if enabled)

These third-party services may collect data in accordance with their own privacy policies. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party platforms.

4. Cookies

This website may use cookies or similar technologies to enhance user experience. Cookies help understand how visitors interact with the site and allow for basic functionality.

You can disable cookies through your browser settings if you prefer.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Children's Privacy

This website is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe that information from a child has been submitted, please contact us and we will remove it promptly.

7. Your Rights and Choices

You may request to:

  • Access the personal information you have provided
  • Request correction or deletion of your information

To make a request, please contact us using the information below.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of the website after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.
